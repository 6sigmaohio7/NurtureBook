# NurtureBook Prompt Library

## Guided Journal Prompts

### Parenting Growth
- "Describe a moment this week where you felt proud as a parent."
- "What challenged you recently and how did you respond?"
- "What do you wish future-you remembers about today?"

### Emotional Awareness
- "Name the strongest emotion you felt today and why."
- "What drained your energy recently, and what restored it?"

### Child Memory Capture
- "Write about a recent moment with your child that felt meaningful."
- "Describe something new your child learned or showed interest in."

## Daily Reflection Format
Instructions to user:
Short, simple, about five sentences maximum.

Examples:
- "Today I appreciated..."
- "Something that surprised me..."
- "Something I'm learning is..."
- "I want to improve by..."
